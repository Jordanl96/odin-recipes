Mac's Famous Mac & Cheese

Description

"Mac calls it "Mac's famous mac and cheese", and it is famous... in my stomach" - Dennis Reynolds

Tell me about Mac's famous mac and cheese? Well, its a homemade classic from none other than the legendary Ronald McDonald (not that one). This is the perfect comfort food after a long day in traffic or fixing the pool filter, or even for a dinner party with friends. If you're feeling like switching it up, it pairs beautifully with tendered hunks of meat.

Ingredients

  • Kraft pre-made Macaroni & Cheese
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 6 cups of water
  • 4 Tbsp butter
  • Hunks of meat (optional)

Steps

  1. Empty packet into a pan
  2. Add in water, milk and butter
  3. Boil until thickened to your liking
  4. Optionally, add in cooked hunks of meat
