Rum Ham

Description

"Oh, this is ham, soaked in rum. It is loaded with booze" - Franklin Reynolds

A delicacy of the guido's of the Jersey Shore, this booze loaded ham is best enjoyed on a syringe filled beach in the sun, or following an extended brining in the ocean.

Ingredients

  • Topside Ham Joint
  • High Quality Carribean Rum
  • Canned Pineapple Slices
  • Sun Block Drink (Chilled)

Steps

  1. Soak ham in rum
  2. Bake at 200 for 2 hours
  3. Baste in extra rum every 30 minutes
  4. Allow to cool, then pour over extra rum as desired
  5. Pin pinapple rings using cocktail sticks, and serve with chilled sun block
